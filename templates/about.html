About ResQConnect

ResQConnect is a emergency assistance platform designed to connect people in distress with immediate help. Whether it's a medical crisis, roadside accident, fire outbreak, or natural disaster, ResQConnect ensures you're never alone.

Our platform provides real-time disaster alerts, first aid information, emergency complaint registration, and access to critical emergency services through a single, user-friendly interface.
